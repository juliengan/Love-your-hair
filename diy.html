For dry skin

Avocado face mask

If you have dry skin, you know that the majority
of dry-skin remedies involve heavy oils. Try this mask to hydrate your dry skin !

Ingredients:

  • 1 avocado
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
